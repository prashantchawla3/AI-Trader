Every time you trade, the broker makes money. So what does the broker incentivize to encourage you to do? They want you to trade so they can make money. So how do they do that? They tell you, "Hey, there's a stock today up 175% on 100 million shares of volume. You should check it out." And so now all of these big brokers send out these daily emails essentially sort of fanning the fire telling you what's moving, giving you some FOMO. Tesla's moving today. Facebook is moving today. Netflix is moving today. Small caps, large caps, overall market, they just kind of keep trying to encourage you to trade because the more you trade, the more money they'll make. Now, this is where it's important. You can't get caught in overtrading. You've got to make sure you don't fall victim to jumping in everything that's moving, but you focus on trading stocks that actually fit within your strategy.
