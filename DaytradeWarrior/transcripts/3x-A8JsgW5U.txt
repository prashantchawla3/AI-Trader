Remember, candlesticks are based on periods of time. So, on a 1-minute time on 1-minute chart, each candle is 1 minute of time. So, every 60 seconds a candle's closing and a new one opens. So, we might only be 10 seconds in the next candle, but if it's diving down and going red, I'll see the current candlestick shape is red and I'll exit my position.
