Demand is certainly created by news, but that's not the only thing that creates demand. Why was it that Tesla went up so much between 2020 and 2022? Yes, the demand was very, very high, but it wasn't like there was news coming out back to back to back to back. What was going on was people were hyped up. People were enthusiastic. People love Tesla. And so you need that degree of people in love with the stock, people who like the stock, people who think the stock has the potential to go higher.
