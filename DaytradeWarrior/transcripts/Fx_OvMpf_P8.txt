Day trading is gambling. For some people, day trading is gambling. Here's the difference. It's gambling when you don't have a strategy, you don't have a set of rules that you follow, and when you're not consistently profitable. So, if you're losing money hand over fist, then you are essentially betting on a game of chance because you have no consistency. But, once you have strategy, once you have that system, that set of rules that you follow every single day, you are taking chance out of the equation.
