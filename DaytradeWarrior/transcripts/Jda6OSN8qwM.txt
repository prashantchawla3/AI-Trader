You may not know this yet, but my strategy is based entirely around trading the volatility that is the result of a stock having breaking news. Candlestick charts and the language of technical analysis that you're learning here today can be applied to any financial instrument, stocks, futures, cryptocurrency, it doesn't matter. I choose to apply it to trading stocks and specifically smaller cap lower price stocks that have the potential, as you can see here, to go up 200, 300, 400% or more in a single day when they have news. These are stocks that typically trade a millions of shares of volume after that news first comes out. So, what we have here was a breaking news event and this first candle, which is green, so this candle opened at the bottom and because it doesn't have a lower candle wick, it means the price never went down.
