Last year on January 1st, I had $110,000 in my day trading account. By the end of the year, I had grown that account to over $1.8 million of fully verified and independently audited trading profits. These are the three steps I followed to grow that account. Step one, I treat trading like a business. I show up every single day, rain or shine, whether it's a hot market, a cold market, and I trade. I took about 5,000 trades over the course of the entire year. My winners averaged about $1,000. My losers were about 600 and I maintained accuracy of just about 65%. Step two, I focused on trading a strategy that I knew was proven profitable. I knew other people were trading it profitably, and it was a strategy that I myself had had success with in the past. Step three, I focused on doubling down on winning trades in hot markets and cutting my losers quickly and scaling back when the market was cold. The philosophy here is breakout or bailout.
