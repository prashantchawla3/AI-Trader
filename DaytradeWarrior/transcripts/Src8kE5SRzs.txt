Being a successful trader means being really good at losing, knowing when to hold them and when to fold them, to use a little expression there, but really most importantly knowing when to walk away. That's part of your edge. That's part of your strategy. You have to mitigate your downside risk. So, when you start losing, you have to recognize that and be like, "No, okay. This is a day where I've got to walk away. I've got to follow the rules of my system and not over trade on this type of day." And on the other hand, on a day when the market's really hot, that's a day to trade longer, to lean in, to be more aggressive. So, this is how you amplify the hot streaks and then keep the cold streaks like really as much to a minimum as possible.
