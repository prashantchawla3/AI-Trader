When it comes to trading in the market, if you follow your natural impulses, you're almost guaranteed to lose money. Your natural impulses will be selling and panicking when something is dropping and jumping in and chasing when something is moving higher. And what we know is that we need to be doing the opposite. We need to be buying off of support with a well-understood level of risk and selling into those big extensions. So, the only way to be consistently profitable is to find a way to override your gut intuition and instead have the discipline and the commitment to follow a strategy.
