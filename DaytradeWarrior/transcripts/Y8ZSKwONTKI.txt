This is a market to survive till you thrive. Just keep your head above water. It's not hot right now, but this is a great opportunity to train yourself to throttle down, to be patient, to wait for good quality setups. Because what happens for a lot of traders is that when the market's hot, the way it was in May, June, and July, they'll make some great money. And then when the market cools off, they keep going full throttle and they give back half of what they made, maybe all of what they made. And then over the course of four, five months, six months, a year, made money, lost money, they've got nothing to show for it. So the key to long-term success in the market is not just about making money. It's about learning when to take your foot off the gas so you lose less when the conditions aren't optimal for your strategy.
