this is a concise beginner's guide to the stock market so how does the stock market work well the stock market is a place where individual investors like you and i can buy fractional ownership of these huge companies like apple or tesla and the reason these companies sell shares onto the open market is because the proceed from those sales goes back into the company and allows them to fund future growth if they realize that growth it'll be reflected as the stock price increases over time if they don't realize that growth the price will go down so why do people flock to the market instead of real estate or other investment opportunities well one of the reasons is because the barrier to entry is so low you can be investor with as little as a dollar a hundred dollars put in the market 20 years ago would be worth 450 dollars today so not only are you beating inflation historically you're also returning a profit which is your reward for taking the risk of investing in these companies
