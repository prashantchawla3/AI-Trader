The way I became a profitable trader was because I had a really big loss. The big loss I had that was the first week of August beginning of the new month, that blew out my account, I was offline, I went back, I studied my metrics, I reviewed all of my trades, what I was doing right, what I was doing wrong, and I came out of that with a game plan of what I was going to do differently. I had a very strict set of rules, and I forced myself to be disciplined. I got a coach, someone who helped me not with trading, but with being a more disciplined person. This is something that it was actually easier to find a coach for that than it was to find a trading coach in those days. So, I found someone that was going to help hold me accountable, like an accountability buddy, just with being disciplined and with being mindful and not letting myself give in to these emotions of anger, fear, and greed.
