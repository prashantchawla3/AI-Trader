The quicker you are at identifying changes in trend, the more money you'll make. You'll be buying before the trend changes or right as it changes from down to up, and you'll be selling just before it changes from up back to down. And what you'll find is that most stocks trade in sort of these waves where they move up and down and up and down. And so you get many opportunities throughout the day to time these entries and exits right around the pivots in price.
